About Me

Learning, building, and sharing my passion for all things geospatial. Over the course of the last decade, I've been fortunate to work as a municipal GIS coordinator and a geo data specialist for both Apple Maps and Google Street View. Currently, I'm completing the first year of my Master's of Geography at the University of Massachusetts. This portfolio site is the capstone project for my WebGIS class. Enjoy!
